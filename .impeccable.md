## Design Context

### Users
Directors and C-suite executives at mid-sized Mexican B2B companies looking for strategic digital consulting. They browse the site on desktop during work hours, evaluating whether Miguel is the right consultant for their digital transformation needs. Secondary audience: digital professionals and marketers who consume his blog content for thought leadership.

### Brand Personality
1. **Strategic** — High-level vision, not tactical execution
2. **Premium** — Every element communicates value and professionalism
3. **Trustworthy** — Serious, proven track record, no fluff

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Light mode with navy primary (#051C2C) and cobalt accent (#1D4ED8)
- **Typography**: Playfair Display (headings, serif, authoritative) + Inter (body, clean sans)
- **Visual tone**: Executive consulting firm, not startup or creative agency. Think McKinsey meets modern web.
- **Anti-references**: No influencer aesthetics, no startup hustle culture, no colorful agency vibes, no generic corporate without personality
- **Photography**: AI-generated professional portraits, low saturation, business context
- **Icons**: Lucide React, outline style, thin stroke

### Design Principles
1. **Authority through restraint** — One accent color (cobalt), generous whitespace, let content breathe
2. **Data over decoration** — Stats, certifications, and results speak louder than visual effects
3. **Progressive disclosure** — Start simple (hero), build complexity (services, calculator, certifications)
4. **Credibility first** — Social proof, credentials, and testimonials positioned high on the page
